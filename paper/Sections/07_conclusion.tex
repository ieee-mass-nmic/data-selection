\section{Conclusion}

PCU-Select treats registry-scale data selection as quality-preserving amortization: structural PEFT and task conditions produce target-specific subsets from one scorer. Across four tasks and five seen configurations, it near-ties per-PEFT LESS while onboarding each new PEFT for $44\times$ less than a rebuilt datastore (1.44 vs 63.2 GPU-hours). Near-support targets transfer zero-shot; calibration repairs far same-family targets and BitFit, while Prefix/P-Tuning remain a failure boundary. PCU-Select is reusable selection infrastructure for a PEFT registry.
